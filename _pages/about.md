<div class="research-intro">
  <p class="research-lead">I am a Ph.D. student in Tumor Biology in the <a href='https://ki.se/en/mtc/wilhelm-lab'>Wilhelm Group</a> at the <a href='https://ki.se/en/mtc/department-of-microbiology-tumor-and-cell-biology'>Department of Microbiology, Tumor and Cell Biology</a>, Karolinska Institutet.</p>

  <p>My work focuses on modeling neuroblastoma using reprogrammed patient-derived stem cells, with the goal of understanding tumor initiation, cell-state transitions, and developmental mechanisms that shape disease progression.</p>
